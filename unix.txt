Find unique file types
find . -type f | perl -ne 'print $1 if m/\.([^.\/]+)$/' | sort | uniq -c
find . -type f | sed -n 's/..*\.//p' | sort | uniq -c

Find lines in file2 that are not in file1
grep -vf file1 file2

Intersect two files
grep -Fxf file1 file2

Diff output between two commands
diff <(cmd a) <(cmd b)
